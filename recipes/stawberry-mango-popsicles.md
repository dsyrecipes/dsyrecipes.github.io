# Stawberry Mango Popsicles

## info  
* 10 minutes
* 8 popsicles

## ingredients
* 1 cup fresh strawberries
* 2 cup mango
* 1 cup orange juice

## steps  
1.  Blend strawberries and 1/4 orange joice until smooth
2.  Add mango chunks and the remaining 3/4 cup orange juice
3.  Pour moulds
4.  Place in freezer for 4 hours
