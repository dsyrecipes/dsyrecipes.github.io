# Crepes  

## info  
* About 10 minutes  
* 5 crepes

## ingredients
* 1 cup flour
* 1/2 cup water
* 1/2 cup milk
* 2 eggs
* 1/4 tsp salt

## steps  
1.  Mix ingredients in mixing bowl
2.  Add oil in pan
3.  Pour batter in pan and make flat
4.  Fry 1 minute on each side
